To change the version of the Game, go tot the GameManager class and go to the method "setUpGameplay.

There is NumberOfRounds enum which are Quickplay(5 rounds), Seven, and Ten. Enter one of the enums for the "this.Rounds = your enum"

There is DollarAmount enums which are the winnings for the game. Quickplay is only for the Quickplay number of rounds and vice-versa.
Regular and Syndicated can be played with the seven or Ten round version of the game. Regular goes up to $1,000,000 in winnings and 
Syndicated goes up to $500,000 in winnings.
